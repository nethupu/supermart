About Us

SuperMart is your one-stop shop for fresh produce, household items, and more. Our dedicated team strives to provide exceptional customer service.

With convenient locations and a user-friendly online platform, shopping at SuperMart is always a breeze.





Our Branches In Sri Lanka

Welcome to SuperMart, your go-to destination for all your shopping needs! With two main branches located in Colombo and Kandy, we are committed to providing you with the best shopping experience.


Colombo Branch

Our Colombo branch, situated in Union Place, offers a modern shopping environment with a wide selection of groceries, fresh produce and much more. Enjoy the convenience of ample parking, friendly customer service, and a variety of international and local products.


Kandy Branch

Our Kandy branch, nestled amidst the scenic beauty of this historic city, provides a unique shopping experience tailored to meet the needs of our customers. With a focus on fresh, locally sourced produce and a range of quality goods, our Kandy store ensures you have everything you need under one roof.
